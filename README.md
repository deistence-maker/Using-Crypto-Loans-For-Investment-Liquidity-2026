## What Is Investment Liquidity in Crypto?

Investment liquidity refers to having access to capital that can be used to fund opportunities without liquidating your existing assets.

Instead of selling your crypto holdings, you borrow against them and use the funds elsewhere.

This allows you to keep your position while still participating in other investments.

---

## How Crypto Loans Enable Investment Liquidity

The process works as follows:

1. You deposit crypto as collateral  
2. The platform calculates your borrowing limit  
3. You receive a loan  
4. You use the funds for investment  
5. You repay the loan to recover your crypto  

This structure allows your crypto to remain intact while still generating financial utility.

## The Double Exposure Effect

When using crypto loans for investment, you are exposed to two risks at the same time:

1. Crypto market risk  
2. Investment performance risk  

If either side performs poorly, your financial position may be affected.

---

## Key Risk: Liquidation

If your collateral value drops significantly, your loan may be liquidated.

This means:

- Your crypto is sold  
- Your loan is repaid automatically

## Safe Strategy for Investment Liquidity

### Use Low LTV

Stay within 20–40% to reduce liquidation risk.

---

### Avoid Speculative Investments

Do not use borrowed funds for high-risk or uncertain opportunities.

---

### Maintain a Safety Buffer

Keep additional collateral available in case of market drops.

---

### Monitor Both Positions

Track:

- Crypto market movements  
- Performance of your investment  

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
